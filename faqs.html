The Basics: Getting to Know Hurrae

What services does Hurrae offer?

Hurrae specialises in Webflow Development, UI/UX Design, and Branding services. We help businesses create visually stunning websites, craft user-friendly interfaces, and develop unforgettable brand identities.

How long has Hurrae been in business?

Hurrae was founded in 2023 by a group of digital enthusiasts with a passion for creating unique and engaging online experiences.

What sets Hurrae apart from other design and development studios?

At Hurrae, we pride ourselves on our quirky, creative approach to digital design and development. Our team is committed to working closely with clients to ensure their visions come to life through innovative solutions, cutting-edge technology, and a keen eye for detail.

What industries or clients do you typically work with?

e work with clients across a wide range of industries, from small startups to large corporations. Our diverse portfolio includes projects in e-commerce, finance, healthcare, education, technology, and more.

Our Services: Delving Deeper into the Magic

Webflow Development
How does the Webflow development process work?

Our Webflow development process starts with a thorough understanding of your project goals and requirements. We then create a custom design and develop the site using Webflow's powerful tools and features. Once the site is complete, we perform rigorous testing to ensure it's fully functional and ready for launch.

Can you help with website maintenance and updates?

Absolutely! We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to help you keep your site up-to-date and running smoothly. Our team can handle everything from content updates to bug fixes and new feature development.

UI/UX Design
What tools and software do you use for UI/UX design?

Our design team uses a variety of tools, including Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, and InVision, to create visually stunning and user-friendly interfaces. We also employ various prototyping and testing tools to ensure our designs meet the highest usability standards.

How do you approach the branding process?

Our branding process starts with a deep dive into your business, target audience, and unique selling points. We then create a visual identity that reflects your brand's personality and values, including logo design, colour schemes, typography, and other visual elements.

Your Project: The Nuts and Bolts of Our Collaboration

How do you estimate project costs and timelines?

We provide custom quotes for each project based on its specific requirements, scope, and complexity. Once we have a clear understanding of your needs, we'll provide you with a detailed proposal outlining the estimated costs and timelines for completion.

What's your project management process like?

Our project management process includes regular communication, progress updates, and milestone check-ins to ensure your project stays on track and within budget. We use tools like Trello, Asana, or Basecamp to manage tasks and collaborate with clients throughout the project lifecycle.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

We believe that open communication and collaboration are key to a successful project. We'll work closely with you during the design and development phases, incorporating your feedback to ensure the final result meets your expectations. We typically include a set number of revision rounds in our proposals, but we're always open to discussing additional revisions if needed.

Can you work with my existing brand guidelines or assets?

Absolutely! If you have existing brand guidelines or assets, we'll be more than happy to work with them. Our team can seamlessly integrate your current branding elements into the new design or development project while maintaining a consistent look and feel across all touchpoints.

Tech Talk: Our Geeky Side Unleashed

What platforms do you develop websites on?

While our primary focus is on Webflow development, we also have experience working with other platforms like WordPress, Shopify, and Squarespace. We'll work with you to choose the best platform based on your project requirements and goals.

Do you design responsive and mobile-friendly websites?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of having a website that looks and performs well on all devices. All our designs are created with a mobile-first approach, ensuring a seamless user experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Can you help with search engine optimization (SEO)?

Yes, we can! We follow best practices for on-page SEO during the design and development process. This includes proper heading structures, meta tags, and optimising images for faster load times. If you require more advanced SEO services, we can also recommend trusted partners to help with your search engine marketing efforts.

What kind of post-project support do you offer?

We offer various support packages tailored to your needs, from basic website maintenance and updates to more comprehensive ongoing support. Our team is dedicated to ensuring your website continues to perform well and stays up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies.

Still have questions or ready to start your project?

If you have any unanswered questions or want to discuss your project, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help and can't wait to hear from you!
